About Us

Welcome to Homemade Pickles & Snacks – where tradition meets taste! We specialize in handcrafting delicious pickles and snacks using age-old recipes passed down through generations. All our products are made in small batches with 100% natural ingredients and absolutely no chemical preservatives.

Our mission is to preserve authentic flavors, empower local women through employment, and promote eco-friendly packaging.

  • ✔️ No chemical preservatives
  • ✔️ Handmade with love
  • ✔️ Organic, locally sourced ingredients
  • ✔️ Unique spice blends for each pickle
  • ✔️ Crunchy, nostalgic South Indian snacks

What makes us different? We don’t mass-produce – we care, we handcraft, and we deliver a touch of home in every bite.
